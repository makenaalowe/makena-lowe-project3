Student + Artist

As a dedicated art student, I've spent years exploring different mediums and styles, constantly learning and evolving as an artist. My journey has been shaped by curiosity and a passion for self-expression. Whether working on traditional or digital art, I love experimenting with color, form, and texture to create unique pieces that tell a story. I'm always seeking new ways to grow, both technically and creatively, and I believe that art is a lifelong learning process.
